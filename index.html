Local-first beta

Chat Margin stores notes, prompt drafts, follow-up status and preferences locally in your browser. It does not require a Chat Margin account and does not send your notes to a Chat Margin server.

Chat Margin is an independent browser extension and is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by OpenAI or ChatGPT.
