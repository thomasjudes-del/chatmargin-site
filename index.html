Product

A local workspace for ChatGPT users.

ChatMargin is a browser extension for people who use ChatGPT across long, complex or unfinished conversations.

  • What it is: a local sidebar that appears next to ChatGPT conversations.
  • What it does: lets you keep notes, prepare prompts and track follow-up conversations.
  • Who it is for: heavy ChatGPT users, consultants, researchers, founders, builders and writers.
  • Current status: free beta, with optional voluntary contributions to support development.
Why it exists

ChatGPT is powerful. Long conversations are messy.

ChatMargin is for people who use ChatGPT as a serious work surface: consultants, builders, researchers, founders, writers and AI-heavy teams.

✏️

Notes per conversation

Capture context, decisions, loose ideas and next steps directly next to the conversation where they belong.

💬

Prompt drafts

Prepare prompts before sending them. Dictate, copy, and keep a simple record of what you already used.

📊

Follow-up dashboard

Mark conversations as unfinished and return to them later from a local dashboard.

Local-first

Your notes stay in your browser.

ChatMargin is designed to be useful without accounts, servers or hidden data collection.

  • Notes and prompt drafts are stored locally in your browser extension storage.
  • There is no ChatMargin account and no ChatMargin server in the beta.
  • We do not sell user data.
  • Feedback is only sent when you deliberately email us.
  • See the Privacy Policy for the full wording.

Free while we build with early users.
